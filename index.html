Associated Student Government (ASG) at Northwestern

Executive Officer of Technology, 2022-2023 Cabinet

Responsibilites: Independently developing The ASG Personnel Accountability Trackr and ASG Pivot

- Currently developing The Personnel Accessibility Tracker (The PAT): allows ASG's Head of Accountability to log weekly progress of ASG Exec members, search data by person, analyze government-wide metrics, and record important notes to keep track of over time
- Currently developing ASG Pivot, a means for Northwestern administrators and students to view and interact with survey data that ASG has collected over the last ten years. The goals of this project are to 1) increase transparency between students and their government, and 2) give students the data required to hold their representatives accountable.
